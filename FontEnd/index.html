Our Story

Ayubowan! Welcome to the Sri Lankan Traditional Workshop 🌸

Step into a world of culture, creativity, and timeless tradition. At our workshop, we celebrate the beauty of Sri Lanka’s rich heritage by bringing people together to experience traditional crafts, art, and skills passed down through generations.

Whether it’s learning the art of weaving, trying your hand at woodcarving, or discovering the stories behind our island’s unique crafts, every moment here is about creating memories while keeping traditions alive.

Our doors are open to everyone – locals, visitors, learners, and enthusiasts – who wish to connect, create, and carry a piece of Sri Lankan tradition with them.
